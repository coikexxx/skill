# Output Template

Use this structure when GEO risk is relevant.

## Short form

- **Risk level**: low / medium / high
- **Reliable parts**: what is supported by stronger evidence
- **Suspect parts**: what looks marketing-driven, recycled, or weakly sourced
- **Confirmed now**: facts that can be stated safely
- **Unverified**: claims that need stronger proof
- **Next step**: what to check next

## Long form

### Initial judgment
State whether the search space appears clean, mixed, or polluted.

### Why
List the main red flags and the strongest evidence.

### Evidence quality
Separate primary evidence, strong secondary evidence, and weak/high-risk evidence.

### Safe conclusion
Give only the conclusion justified by the strongest evidence.

### Residual uncertainty
Say what remains unresolved and why.

### Practical recommendation
Tell the user how to verify further or how to ask the next question more safely.
